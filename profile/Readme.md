## Overview

Covera is a modern, cloud-based insurance management platform designed to streamline the entire insurance lifecycle. From policy administration and claims processing to customer management and regulatory reporting, Covera provides insurers, brokers, and underwriters with a single integrated platform to manage their operations efficiently and at scale.

Built for insurance professionals by insurance professionals, our platform handles motor, health, life, and property insurance products with speed, security, and reliability.

## Core Modules

| Module | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Policy Administration** | Create, manage, and track insurance policies with automated renewals, endorsements, cancellations, and policy document generation. |
| **Claims Management** | End-to-end claims processing from First Notice of Loss (FNOL) to settlement, with automated validation, fraud detection, and real-time status tracking. |
| **Customer Management** | Comprehensive customer profiles with KYC verification, communication history, relationship management, and beneficiary tracking. |
| **Premium Calculation** | Automated premium calculations with flexible rate cards, risk assessment tools, and dynamic pricing models. |
| **Payment Processing** | Integrated payment gateway for premium collection, claims payments, and refunds with support for M-Pesa and card payments. |
| **Analytics & Reporting** | Real-time dashboards, custom reports, predictive analytics, and performance metrics for data-driven decision making. |
| **Renewal Automation** | Auto-renewal workflows, renewal reminders, and endorsement tracking to reduce policy lapse rates. |
| **Regulatory Compliance** | Built-in reporting for IRA (Insurance Regulatory Authority of Kenya) requirements and statutory filings. |

## Insurance Products Supported

| Product Line | Capabilities |

- **Fraud Detection Utilities** — Open-source tools for claims fraud pattern detection and risk scoring
